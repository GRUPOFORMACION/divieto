Est. Miami, Florida

"La cucina è arte.
Il divieto, leggenda."

Divieto — Italian for "prohibition" — is a love letter to two worlds: the timeless craft of Italian cuisine and the bold spirit of 1920s America. More than 70 dishes crafted from scratch, each one a celebration of local ingredients, old-world technique and the kind of warmth that turns dinner into memory.

The Atmosphere

A Room That Speaks

Velvet, candlelight and the low murmur of satisfied guests. Every corner pays homage to the Prohibition era — intimate booths, dark wood, and art-deco details that make every evening feel like a scene from a classic film.
