Our Story

Where Italian tradition meets American legend

The Name

Divieto. Italian for Prohibition.

In 1920s America, Prohibition didn't silence the desire for beauty, pleasure, or great company — it only drove them underground. Secret bars. Whispered invitations. Tables set in hidden rooms. The era gave birth to a culture of defiance wrapped in elegance.

Divieto Ristorante was born from that same spirit: a place where the rules of ordinary dining don't apply, where the food is bold, the atmosphere immersive, and every table feels like it was set just for you.

The Concept

Italian Soul.
American Heart.

Our menu draws a straight line from the trattorias of Bologna to the steakhouses of Manhattan — two traditions that have more in common than they admit. We honour both without compromising either.

More than 70 dishes are crafted entirely from scratch every day, using locally sourced produce and imported Italian pantry staples. From our housemade pasta to our dry-aged cuts, every element is made with intention.

The Signature

The Ruota Ritual

No dish defines Divieto more than the Ruota di Parmigiano. A wheel of 30-month aged Parmigiano-Reggiano, brought to your table. Our chef finishes fettuccine Alfredo directly inside the wheel, the heat melting the cheese into the pasta in real time.

It's not just a dish. It's a moment — one of those rare restaurant experiences that people describe to their friends long after the meal is over.

The Space

Designed to Linger

Each Divieto location is designed as a place you don't want to leave. Art-deco details, warm light, deep booths, and a bar program inspired by the legendary speakeasies of the 1920s create an environment that turns dinner into an evening.

Four locations across South Florida — North Miami Beach, Doral, Estero, and Aventura — each with its own character, all sharing the same commitment to excellence.
